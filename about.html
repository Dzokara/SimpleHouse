History of our restaurant

In 2010, four college friends - Jeniffer, Daisy, Florence, and Valentina - were studying in a big city on the West Coast. They were always busy with classes and extracurricular activities, and often found themselves craving fast food late at night.

One night, they decided to start their own fast food delivery service, called "Simple House" to bring their favorite fast food to hungry college students like themselves. They pooled their savings, bought a used van, and started making deliveries around campus.
